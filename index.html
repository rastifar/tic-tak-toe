How to Play Tic-Tac-Toe

  1. Start the Game: Click on any tile on the game board to begin.
  2. Player Turns: Each time you click on a tile, the player role alternates between Player X and Player O.
  3. Objective: The goal is to get three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) before your opponent does.

Enjoy the game and may the best player win!
